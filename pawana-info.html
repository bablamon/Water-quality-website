Pawana River Water Quality & Sanitization

Pawana River - Water Quality Information

Water Hardness: Moderate hardness levels, which makes it less suitable for drinking directly without treatment.

Sanitization Methods: Filtration methods like activated carbon filters and reverse osmosis (RO) can be used to purify the water. Boiling is also recommended for basic sanitization.
