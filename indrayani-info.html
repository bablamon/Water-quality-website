Indrayani River Water Quality & Sanitization

Indrayani River - Water Quality Information

Water Hardness: High hardness levels, which can cause scaling in pipes and appliances. It's unsuitable for drinking without proper treatment.

Sanitization Methods: Due to high hardness, advanced filtration is recommended, such as using Reverse Osmosis (RO) systems. Boiling is also necessary to eliminate pathogens. Chlorine or UV filtration can also be considered for better purification.
