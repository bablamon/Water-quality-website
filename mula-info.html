Mula River Water Quality & Sanitization

Mula River - Water Quality Information

Water Hardness: Slightly hard water, making it more suitable for consumption but still needs some form of treatment.

Sanitization Methods: UV purification systems are effective for this river's water. Reverse Osmosis (RO) is another good option to ensure the water is safe for drinking. Boiling can also help in killing harmful microorganisms.
